# Code Of Conduct

This project expects respectful, technically grounded collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Focus criticism on code, design, and evidence.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Repeated bad-faith argument or deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without consent

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, reject contributions, or remove participants who ignore these expectations.
